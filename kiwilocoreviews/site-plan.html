Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a one-stop shop for people to leave reviews and suggestions for Kiwi Loco.

Audience

The audience will be customers of Kiwi Loco, as well as a page dedicated to showing other stats.

Dynamic elements

A form with three dropdowns will dynamically present tailored review forms depending on the selection. Required fields will change based on the type of review. Results will be displayed on an admin-only page. A basic login system may be implemented to access this page. A dark mode toggle may also be added.

Review Page

This page contains three dropdown options that display different forms depending on the user's selection:

  • Review the Froyo
  • Review the Customer Service
  • Leave a Suggestion

Review the Froyo

Questions:

  • How many flavors did you get? (required) — This determines how many times the following two questions are repeated.
  • What flavor did you get? (required) — Dropdown menu
  • How would you rate that flavor? (required)
  • How many toppings did you get? (required) — Works the same way as flavors
  • How would you rate that topping? (required)

Review the Customer Service

Questions:

  • What time did you come to our store? (required) — Time picker
  • On a scale of 1-10 how friendly were the employees? (required)
  • On a scale of 1-10 how helpful were the employees? (required)
  • On a scale of 1-10 how likely are you to come back to Kiwi Loco? (required)
  • Any other comments? — Text field

Leave a Suggestion

Questions:

  • Suggest a flavor — Text field
  • Suggest a topping — Text field
